About the game

There are multiple activities in Destiny 2 ranging from PvE content to PvPE. A couple of them include:

Vanguard Strikes These are short PvE activities that require you to complete certain list of objectives in a fireteam of 3 people
Raids Probably the best activity in the game, these require a 6 people to work together and beat the bosses through their unique mechanics
Gambit This is an activity with 2 teams competing against each other while the competing teams try to sabotage the other team by either spawning strong enemies or going to invade to kill people.
Crucible Your standard PvP content with different gamemodes
Battlegrounds You take down one of Caiatl's warchiefs in this activity with a fireteam of 3 people
Override A 6 player activity, in which you hack into a vex system to gather information about the movements of the vex.
Exotic Quests Quests of exotic weapons, usually harder than a strike but easier than a dungeon.
Dungeons An activity made out of 3-5 encounters with unique loot, aesthetic and difficulty
