The game

Destiny 2 is a looter-shooter MMORPG, with different gamemodes and a lot of interesting quests and activites. It's developed by Bungie There are 3 main classes in the game, Titan, Hunter and Warlock. Each of them has their own strengths and weaknesses, with 4 subclasses each.

The story

The Red War Campaign

The Red War Campaign is the main story arc for the game Destiny 2 and revolves around trying to regain your powers while also dealing with the Red Legion and trying to reunite the Vanguard and take back the Last City. The villain of this campaign is Dominus Ghaul, a Cabal conqueror.

Beyond Light Campaign

This campaign is about using the Darkness, your greatest enemy as a weapon against the Eliksni, or Fallen on Europa. You slowly start to gain newfound powers, that you can use to take down your enemies, as well as the main villain of the story, Eramis. She is a Fallen Warlord, who found a way to use Stasis, which is the Darkness's element. She wants to use it to destroy the Light, which made her and her friends suffer for a long time. You are there to stop that.

Things to do in Destiny 2

There are a lot of stuff to do in Destiny 2. Mainly PvE content, but there are some PvP or PvEP gamemodes in the game, called Crucible (PvP) and Gambit (PvEP) the new seasonal content added to the game called Battlegrounds, where you take down a Cabal Warchief in a longer mission. The loot you get from it is awesome, and the weapons are pretty good too.
